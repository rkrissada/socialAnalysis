Nagaoka Summer School for Young Engineers 2018 We would like to make an announcement on “ Nagaoka Summer School for Young Engineers 2018 ” organized by Nagaoka University of Technology ( NUT ). The program offers students the opportunity to participate in research on topics set by the laboratories at NUT , as well as experience cultural exchange from activities with NUT students . The program period will start from 20 August to 31 August 2018 This program is free of charge for successful applicants . In addition , NUT will provide each participant a scholarship for 40,000 JPY and accommodation in Nagaoka during the program . Who should apply : 3 rd year undergraduate students who study in the field of Mechanical Engineering , Electrical Engineering , Materials Science , Civil Engineering , Environmental Engineering . Bioengineering , Information and Management Systems Engineering and Nuclear System Safety Engineering . Up to 20 candidates will be selected . Application : Students who are interested in this program must submit application documents at International Affairs Office , Building 3 ( Next to the Dean ’ s Office ) within 15 March 2013 and later send the application documents via post within 13 April 2018 ( The documents must arrived by this date ) The application documents are 1 ) Application Form ( download from : http://www.nagaokaut.ac.jp/e/kokusai/nassye.html) 2 ) Certificate of Enrollment 3 ) Academic Transcript 4 ) Recommendation Letter and 5 ) Copy of the English language proficiency test ( TOEFL , TOEIC , or IELTS ). Additional Info : Selected candidates are required to purchase their air-tickets to / from Japan and health insurance at their own expenses .
